My favorite locations and vacation spots

These are the places I like to go

The Grand Canyon

  • It's always warm during the day yet surprising cool during the evening and night
  • Train rides are available along the edge of the canyon
  • Overall a pretty nice place

Hawaii

  • Absolutely beautiful
  • Lots of attractions to see
  • Be warned, its very expensive to stay there. It's absolutely worth the cost though
  • The islanders are extremely festive, especially durring New Year's Eve. They'll lauch off so many fireworks that night becomes day for a few seconds
Camp Merriwether
  • It's right next to the Pacific ocean, close enough to hear the waves on the beach
  • Several activities available at the campsite ranging from rock-climbing to archery and even has a blackpowder gun-range
  • Used consistently by the BoyScouts organization as a summer camp
